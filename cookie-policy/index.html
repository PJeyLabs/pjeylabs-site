This Cookie Policy explains how PJey Labs uses cookies and similar storage or access technologies on this website. It should be read with our Privacy Policy.

1. What Cookies Are

Cookies are small text files a website stores on your computer, phone, or other device. Similar technologies include local storage, software development kits, pixels, and other methods that store information on or access information from a device. They can make a site work, remember choices, measure use, or support advertising.

2. Categories We Use

Necessary technologies support core operation, security, and privacy choices. They do not require consent where they are strictly necessary for a service you request.

Functional technologies remember optional choices or enable enhanced features. They are used only with consent where the law requires it.

Analytics technologies measure visits and interactions so a site operator can understand and improve use. They are used only after consent.

Marketing technologies support advertising, conversion measurement, profiling, or cross-site tracking. They are used only after consent.

If optional technologies are introduced, this page will identify their provider, purpose, information used, and duration. The consent tool keeps functional, analytics, and marketing categories disabled until you actively choose them.

4. Your Consent Choices

On your first visit, you can select Accept All, Reject Non-Essential, or Cookie Settings. Rejecting optional technologies does not prevent access to the website.

You can change or withdraw consent at any time by opening . Withdrawing consent does not affect processing that was lawful before withdrawal. Clearing site data in your browser will remove the stored choice and cause the banner to appear again.

5. Browser Controls

Most browsers allow you to inspect, block, or delete cookies and site data through privacy or site settings. Blocking all storage may stop the site from remembering your consent choice. Instructions vary by browser; consult the help pages for Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge, or your chosen browser.
